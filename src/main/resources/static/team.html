Our G20 Techsprint Team

Dr. Kimmo Soramäki Founder & CEO

A former central banker, Kimmo developed the world's first payment system simulator in 1997 at the Bank of Finland, and was among the first to apply network theory methods to financial risk analysis while at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York in 2004.

After the Financial Crisis of 2007-08 he advised several financial authorities, including the European Central Bank and the Bank of England. This work lead him to found FNA in 2014 to provide tools and techniques for financial authorities to map and model the complex financial networks that surround us.

Dr. Samantha Cook Chief Scientist

Samantha (Sam) earned her PhD in statistics at Harvard University and worked in the Research Group at Google before joining FNA in 2014. She has also taught at Columbia University in New York and Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona published in statistics, economics, finance and psychology.

At FNA, Sam works on the design, testing, and implementation of new models and algorithms.

Ivana Ruffini MD, Advanced Analytics

Prior to FNA, Ivana worked for 9 years at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago where she developed models and quantitative surveillance of financial institutions. She also worked for Baird Capital Partners, One Equity Partners and JPMorgan.

At FNA, Ivana manages a team of data scientists and works with clients to help them maximize tangible business outcomes.
